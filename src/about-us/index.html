About Us

We're a new government agency created after the financial crisis of 2008. We write and enforce rules to ensure that markets for consumer financial products and services are fair and open, and to make financial information easier to understand. We also build tools and resources that help arm consumers with the information they need, and empower consumers to make the best financial decisions.
