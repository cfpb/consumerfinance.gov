Voluntary diversity assessment onboarding form

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau’s voluntary diversity assessment onboarding form is the first step in allowing financial institutions to submit diversity and inclusion assessments online.

Please complete and submit this form to gain access to the Inclusivity Portal where you will be able to submit diversity and inclusion assessments to the Bureau. For questions concerning this form, please contact the Office of Minority and Women Inclusion’s Diversity Assessments Support Team at OMWI_diversityassessments@cfpb.gov.
