Privacy Act statement

5 U.S.C. 552(a)(e)(3)

Information you provide to Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (“CFPB”), Office of Financial Education, such as your name, address, email address, and or other contact information, will be used for the purpose of responding to your request for information on CFPB projects or initiatives, and for inclusion into the mailing list that will be used in future communications.

Information collected will be treated in accordance with the System of Records Notice (“SORN”), CFPB.021 – CFPB Consumer Education and Engagement Records, 77 F.R. 60382 . Direct identifying information will only be used to facilitate the initiative, to provide you with tailored updates about the work of the Office of Financial Education, and will be kept private except as required by law.

This collection of information is authorized by Pub. L. No. 111-203 , Title X, Sections 1013 and 1022, codified at 12 U.S.C. §§ 5493 and 5512 .

Submission of this information is voluntary, and you may withdraw participation at any time. You are not required to submit or provide any identifying information; however, not doing so may result in the CFPB being unable to fulfill your request.
