How do your transportation options fit your budget, schedule, and lifestyle?

There are many ways you could get to work — some options are faster, cheaper, or more convenient.

You'll be able to choose a type of transportation and enter how much it costs. If you're unsure about the specifics, don't worry — you can select "I'm not sure" and the item will be added to your to-do list, which you'll receive at the end. Once you finish your first transportation option, you can compare it to other options to help you figure out the best first choice and backup plan.
