Can I afford my payment?

Skipping monthly loan payments can cause financial and legal problems. Default—missing too many payments—can result in low credit scores, additional fees, wage garnishment, and ineligibility for further federal student aid. This page gives you two ways to estimate the affordability of your projected monthly payment.

Your payment will depend on your total debt and your repayment plan. (This tool assumes a standard plan, where you pay the same amount every month for 10 years; other plans with lower monthly payments (and higher total costs of borrowing) are available.)
