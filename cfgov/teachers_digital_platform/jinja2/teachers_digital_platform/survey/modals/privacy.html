Privacy Notice

The Youth Financial Capability Assessment is provided by the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau) as a financial education resource for parents, educators, and students. No information, including personally identifiable information (PII), will be collected by the Bureau. After completing assessment questions there is an optional feature to share assessment results, with the option to include the initials of your name when sharing the assessment results. If you choose to share assessment results, with or without your initials, this information will be transmitted but the Bureau will not collect, view, or maintain any of your PII.

Using this tool and sharing assessment results is voluntary.
